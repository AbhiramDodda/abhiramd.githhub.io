Launch of Open Source Pool

November 2023

As Head of Training and Development, I launched Open Source Pool, a transformative initiative that improved how AAC shares its projects and research insights with the wider community. This platform showcases cutting-edge projects and research developments from our talented club members, fostering a culture of open collaboration and knowledge sharing. The initiative has garnered significant recognition from faculty members and substantially expanded AAC's influence in the technical community. In its inaugural year (2023-24), Open Source Pool successfully launched over 30 diverse projects.
